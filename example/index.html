Biography

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Statistical Science at UCL. I work under the supervision of Prof. Serge Guillas and in partnership with the UK Met Office to research Bayesian calibration processes for the next generation of exascale simulation models. Consequently, I have experience working with large volumes of data from satellite observations to weather/climate model simulation outputs.

My statistical expertise include Gaussian Process emulation and Bayesian calibration and I am always keen to experiment with the latest developments in computational packages. Much of my work now involves using Jax and GPJax, see my GitHub profile to see my project involving these packages.
